Size Guide

All items are available in sizes from children's 10-11 to adult XXL. Vests are offered in both men's and women's specific sizes.

When is Kit Required?

The club vest is mandatory for all sanctioned track and cross-country competitions. We strongly encourage wearing club kit at all events, including road races.
