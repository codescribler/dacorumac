Record Eligibility

Club recognised best performances may be set by our paid-up first claim members whilst representing our club or their school at County level, or their college, their university, their country or above.

  • Wind-assisted performances in sprints/jumps must be within legal limits (max 2.0 m/s)
  • Indoor performances are marked with (i)
  • Road courses must be certified for records to count
