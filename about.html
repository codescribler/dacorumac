Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

Dacorum Athletics Club was formed in 1995 through the merger of two established clubs: Dacorum AC (founded 1976) and Tring AC (founded 1980). This union brought together decades of athletics experience and passion, creating one of Hertfordshire's premier athletics clubs.

Today, we're proud to serve over 300 members ranging from Year 5 youngsters taking their first steps in athletics to Masters athletes in their 70s and beyond. Our diverse membership reflects our belief that athletics truly is for everyone.

With 31 qualified coaches, including 5 former international athletes, we offer expert guidance across all disciplines. Our athletes regularly compete at county, regional, national, and international levels.

1976

Dacorum AC Founded

The original Dacorum Athletics Club is established in Hemel Hempstead.

1980

Tring AC Established

Tring Athletic Club begins serving athletes in the western Dacorum area.

1995

The Merger

Dacorum AC and Tring AC merge to form the modern Dacorum Athletics Club.

Today

300+ Members Strong

From social runners to world record holders, we continue to grow.

World-Class Facilities

We train at Jarman Park Athletics Track in Hemel Hempstead, a modern 8-lane synthetic track with full field event facilities.

  • 8-lane all-weather synthetic track
  • Long jump and triple jump pits
  • High jump and pole vault areas
  • Throws circle and caged throwing area
  • Floodlighting for evening training
  • Changing facilities and clubhouse
