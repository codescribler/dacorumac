Overview

The Ultimate Winter Sport

Cross country running is where athletics meets adventure. Every race is different - from muddy woodland trails to windswept parks, each course offers unique challenges.

Our cross country season runs from October to March, with league races, county championships, regional competitions, and national events. It's the perfect complement to track and road running - and the best way to build endurance over winter.

Team Spirit

Every Place Counts

In cross country, team scoring means every runner makes a difference. Your finishing position adds to the team total - the club with the lowest score wins!

Essential Gear

Kit Requirements

Cross country running requires specific kit to handle varied terrain and weather conditions. Here's what you'll need:

Club Vest (Required)

You must wear the Dacorum AC vest to score for the team

Cross Country Spikes

Essential for grip on muddy terrain - 9-15mm spikes recommended

Warm Layers

Bring plenty of warm clothing for before and after races

Spare Shoes & Clothes

You will get muddy! Bring a change of everything

Pro Tips for Race Day

  • 1.

    Arrive at least 45 minutes before your race to register, warm up, and recce the course

  • 2.

    Bring safety pins for your race number (they're often in short supply!)

  • 3.

    Set up a team camp with your clubmates - look for the DAC banner

  • 4.

    Stay to cheer on other club members - your support matters!

  • 5.

    Don't forget a flask of hot drink and snacks for afterwards
