Overview

Run With Friends

Our road running group is all about community and encouragement. Whether you're taking your first steps into running or training for your tenth marathon, you'll find supportive training partners here.

We offer group runs at various paces, structured training sessions, and the opportunity to compete in local races wearing the club vest. Running is always better with friends!
